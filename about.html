About

Hi, I’m Paulina — a UX/UI Designer and Product Designer based in Kraków, Poland.

I design digital products end-to-end, from problem discovery and UX strategy to high-fidelity UI and implementation support. I specialize in creating scalable, system-driven solutions, including Design Systems and component libraries for complex products. My work focuses on building clear, consistent, and reliable user experiences, especially in complex or high-impact environments where structure, scalability, and collaboration across teams are essential. I enjoy working closely with product managers and developers to ensure design decisions translate smoothly into production.

With a background in graphic design as well as project and team management, I balance user needs, business goals, and technical constraints. I’m particularly interested in products that prioritize thoughtful decision-making, long-term consistency, and meaningful user value over surface-level polish.
